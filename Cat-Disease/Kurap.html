Your cat has

Ringworm Disease/ Dermatophytosis

Microsporum canis, Trichophyton mentagrophytes (Fungi)

This disease is a fungal infection of the skin. Typically it results in a red, itchy, scaly, circular rash. Hair loss may occur in the area affected. Symptoms begin four to fourteen days after exposure. Multiple areas can be affected at a given time. Ringworm infections that caused by Trichophyton is zoonotic, meaning that it can also infect humans, so it's best to leave your cat to the vet to prevent transmission of the disease.
