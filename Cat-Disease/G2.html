Cat-Disease?

Does your cat have anemia?

Usually cats experience lethargy, mental changes, increased respiratory effort, pale mucous membranes, increased heart rate and laboratory tests (when PCV is less than 31%, Hgb is less than 10.9 g / dL, and RBC is less than 6.9 mill / uL)
